# Energy Benchmarking Notes

## Energy Intensity

Energy use intensity is annual energy divided by floor area. It is useful for
screening similar buildings, but comparisons should be grouped by building type,
operating schedule, climate, and data quality.

## Outliers

High outliers can indicate retrofit potential, unusual occupancy, metering
errors, missing floor area, or process loads. Low outliers can indicate efficient
operation or incomplete data.

## Benchmarks

Benchmarks are best used as directional signals. A synthetic benchmark should be
labelled as synthetic and should not be presented as a regulatory threshold.
