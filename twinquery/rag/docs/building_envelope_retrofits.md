# Building Envelope Retrofits

## Envelope Signals

Older buildings with high energy intensity often warrant envelope review,
especially when they use combustion heating fuels. Useful indicators include
construction age, window type, roof condition, air leakage complaints, and high
winter heating demand.

## Typical Actions

Envelope retrofit options include air sealing, attic or roof insulation, wall
insulation where practical, window repair or replacement, weatherstripping, and
thermal bridge mitigation. Measures should be sequenced with ventilation review
so indoor air quality is maintained.

## Caveats

Envelope measures can have long paybacks when energy prices are low or access is
difficult. A screening tool should present uncertainty and recommend audits for
capital planning decisions.
