# Retrofit Guidelines

## Screening Principles

Retrofit screening should start with observed building attributes: age, use type,
floor area, heating fuel, energy intensity, emissions, and local climate. A high
priority score should be treated as a triage signal, not a final engineering
decision.

## Common Measures

Common first-pass measures include air sealing, roof and wall insulation,
controls optimization, LED lighting, heat recovery ventilation, and heat pump
feasibility assessment. Recommendations should cite the evidence used and avoid
claiming precise savings without a model, audit, or measured benchmark.

## Local Workflow

For a local digital-twin workflow, keep source data, assumptions, generated SQL,
retrieved guidance, and final answers inspectable. Synthetic demo data must be
clearly labelled as synthetic.
