# Heat Pump Notes

## Candidate Buildings

Heat pump feasibility is often strongest for buildings with electric-ready
infrastructure, aging heating equipment, moderate heating loads, or planned
envelope upgrades. Buildings using heating oil or propane may show higher
emissions reduction potential when switching to efficient electric systems.

## Design Considerations

Cold-climate performance, distribution system compatibility, electrical service
capacity, backup heat strategy, and maintenance capability should be assessed
before recommending electrification.

## Portfolio Screening

At portfolio scale, heat pump screening should combine fuel type, emissions,
energy intensity, equipment age proxies, and local grid emissions factors.
